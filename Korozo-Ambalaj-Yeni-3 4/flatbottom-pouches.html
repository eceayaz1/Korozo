Recyclable Flat Bottom Pouches

Engineered for Perfect Shelf Appeal

We continue to develop environment friendly innovative products as part of our corporate sustainability vision. In this regard, we are delighted to present the newest member of our sustainable packaging range, KORORCY Flat Bottom Pouch, which will meet the needs and expectations of our business partners.

The new KORORCY Flat Bottom Pouch can be used for a variety of products from pet food to confectionery and instant beverages in food industry, from agricultural products to cleaning and personal care products in non-food industry. It is side gusseted with five main panels and offers good product stability and and shelf appeal.

With additional options such as multiple web, re-closure systems and easy carry handle, the new recyclable flat bottom pouches are suitable for products with a weight of 150 g to 10 kg.
