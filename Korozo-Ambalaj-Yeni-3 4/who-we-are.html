KOROZO - A Major Player In The Global Flexible Packaging Market

Flexible Packaging Made for Food and Non-Food Items

Celebrating 50 Years of success, Korozo Group has grown to become one of Turkey’s leading powers in flexible packaging and film industry to around 75 countries worldwide.

We are a reliable, one-stop shop for flexible packaging with the largest manufacturing facilities in the Middle East and Europe with 8 factories having total production area of 201.698 m2 and more than 135.000 tons of production capacity.

KOROZO is committed to sustainable practices to minimise its impact on the environment. France, and Russia to meet the growing overseas demand for our flexible packaging 82% of our production output is exported to Europe. The company strives to make the most efficient use of natural resources, to reduce waste, complies with environmental laws and regulations and supports recycling and re-use activities.
