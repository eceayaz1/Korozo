Packaging for Dairy Products

Cheese & Dairy

Dairy products come in all shapes and sizes and require a multitude of packaging solutions – luckily you don’t have to look any further as we’re sure to have the solution for you.

One requirement across nearly every dairy food product is that they need to be kept chilled. This in itself presents challenges for the packaging to ensure the integrity and freshness of the food is maintained whilst still allowing consumer access.

Cheese is available in many shapes, from blocks to sliced or grated – plus of course soft cheeses. Cream and milk require quite different solutions, but with very strong sealing properties.

Our spout pouches are another great option when packaging liquids – especially milk. They offer flexibility, easy consumer access, exceptional shelf life, freshness and high barrier protection. Similarly, their heat stability, strength and suitability for high speed form, fill and seal packing lines make them an unrivalled choice. On-shelf presence and customer convenience are also important aspects well served by our pouches.
